# 🥏 Jax Disc Golf Weekend

**Jacksonville, FL** · **The Sarge** · **4 Courses** · **3 Days**

Friday – Sunday · Summerville → Jacksonville → Summerville

-----

## Courses at a Glance

|Course            |Location           |Holes         |Cost      |Notes                                                                                                |
|------------------|-------------------|--------------|----------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
|**The Sarge**     |Hardeeville, SC    |18            |Free      |Wooded + water hazards. Concrete pads. ~2 hrs from Summerville.                                      |
|**New World DGC** |Cecil Field, Jax   |38 (4 layouts)|Free      |DGPT Championship history. No pro shop — bring water & discs.                                        |
|**Fore Palms DGC**|Ed Austin Park, Jax|18 + 6 bonus  |Free      |3 tee pads per hole. Multiple pin positions. Top 5 in FL.                                            |
|**The Grove**     |Orange Park, FL    |18            |$10/person|Country club feel. Turf pads. **Book tee time at [GroveDiscGolf.com](https://www.grovediscgolf.com)**|


> ⚠️ **New World** no longer has a pro shop, restaurant, or trash cans. Pack water, snacks, and a trash bag.
> 
> ⚠️ **The Grove** is in a gated community. No reservation = no entry. Book tee times now.
> 
> ⚠️ **Fore Palms** gets crowded on weekends — arrive early.
